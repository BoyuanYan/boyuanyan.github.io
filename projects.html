I. Project cooperated with companies

Self-Optimization Optical Network (SOON) testbed 01/2018 – 12/2019    Cooperate with Huawei and State Grid Corporation of China
  • China State Grid Corp Science and Technology Project (5210ED180047)
  • SOON is a testbed to introduce AI technology into optical network to improve performance of optical network in network engineering and transmission engineering. The implementation of SOON is based on ONOS controller and TensorFlow platform. SOON has been demonstrated at European Conference on Optical Communication in Roma, which is largely the most voted project.
  • I am leader of this project. I design the architecture of SOON, and implement main part of SOON.
Research about Deep-Reinforcement-Learning-based resource allocation algorithm in optical transport network 10/2018 - 10/2019    Cooperate with ZTE
  • Improve performance of current heuristic resource allocation algorithms by using reinforcement learning in several scenarios.
  • I am leader of this project.
Abstraction and Control of Traffic engineered networks 09/2015 – 12/2016    Cooperate with Huawei, Ericsson, Spain Telefonica, China Mobile, China Unicom, Korea SK Telecom, Korea ETRI, and CAICT
  • The objective aimed to promote the standardization process of PCEP protocol stack and Restconf protocol in IETF, and evaluate abstraction and control of traffic engineered networks (ACTN) architecture.
  • We published an IETF standard and several IETF drafts, attended IETF 96th and 97th meetings, and won Best Input Award. And ACTN becomes part of ONOS.
  • I am vice-leader of this project. I’m involved in implementation of physical optical network controller and multi-domain service coordinator, and inter-operation with other controllers developed by partners.
A Unified Controller for Multi-domain Multi-vendor Multi-granularity OTN Networks 09/2014 – 11/2015    Cooperate with China Telecom, Huawei, ZTE and Fiberhome
  • The objective aimed to test SDN architecture on multi-domain multi-vendor multi-granularity optical networks.
  • This project is the first SDON case that provided Internet service for Suning Corporation.
  • I participated in this project. I’m involved in optical extension on multi-domain SDN controller.
